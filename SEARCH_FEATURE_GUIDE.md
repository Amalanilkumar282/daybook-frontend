## â“ FAQ

### Q: Will my search be saved?
**A:** Currently, searches are cleared when you collapse the panel. This ensures you always start fresh.

### Q: Can I search by multiple fields at once?
**A:** Yes! The search box looks across descriptions, IDs, nurse names, and client names simultaneously.

### Q: How do I go back to recent entries?
**A:** Simply click the panel header to collapse, or click "Clear All" button.

### Q: Does search work offline?
**A:** No, search requires an active internet connection to query the server.

### Q: Can I export search results?
**A:** Not yet, but this is a planned feature! For now, you can view and take action on results directly.

### Q: What does the yellow highlighting mean?
**A:** Yellow highlighting shows where your search term appears in the results, making it easy to spot matches.

### Q: Can I use wildcards or regex?
**A:** Currently, search is simple text matching. Advanced pattern matching may come in future updates.
